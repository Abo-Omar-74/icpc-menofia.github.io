# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are answers to some common questions about the ICPC Menofia Community.

<details>
<summary>What is the ICPC Menofia Community?</summary>
<p>The ICPC Menofia Community is a student-run organization at Menofia University dedicated to promoting competitive programming. We organize training sessions, workshops, and local contests to help students develop their problem-solving and algorithmic skills for competitions like the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC).</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Who can join the community?</summary>
<p>Our community is open to all students of Menofia University, regardless of their faculty or academic year. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced competitive programmer, you are welcome to join us.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How can I join the community and participate in activities?</summary>
<p>Joining is easy! The best way to get involved is by joining our <a href="https://discord.gg/bdKQ3RSSxK" target="_blank">Discord Community</a>. All our announcements, training schedules, and discussions happen there. You can also follow our social media pages to stay updated.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>I'm new to programming. Is this community for me?</summary>
<p>Absolutely! We have a dedicated <strong>Newcomer Training</strong> track designed to guide you from the very basics of programming to the fundamentals of competitive programming. We believe in building a strong foundation and provide ample support for beginners.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What programming languages are primarily used?</summary>
<p>The most common language in competitive programming, and in our community, is <strong>C++</strong> due to its performance and extensive Standard Template Library (STL). However, problem-solving skills are language-agnostic, and you are free to use other languages like Java or Python. Most of our training materials will be in C++.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How is the training structured?</summary>
<p>Our training is divided into multiple levels: <strong>Newcomer, Level 1, Level 2, and Senior</strong>. Each level has a structured plan covering specific topics, from basic data structures and algorithms to advanced techniques. This ensures a clear learning path for all members. You can find more details on our <a href="/training/">Training page</a>.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Are there any fees to join or participate in events?</summary>
<p>No, being a part of the ICPC Menofia Community and participating in our regular training sessions and workshops is completely <strong>free</strong>. Some official contests or special events might have their own registration fees, but community activities are open to all members at no cost.</p>
</details>
